# BIE Hero — Creative Vision

> The Intelligence Engine is not the object in the middle.  
> The Intelligence Engine is the entire environment surrounding the visitor.

This document is the **creative north star** for the landing-page BIE hero.  
Implementation follows **milestones** — not a single “add more elements” pass.

## What we are designing

The visual representation of an **autonomous intelligence** — not a website animation, hero illustration, or logo.

When a user lands here, they should feel they have entered the **living operating system** that powers BlackOut. Not: “that’s a cool animation.”

## Core philosophy

BIE is the **operating brain** of BlackOut. It constantly observes, validates, reasons, learns, improves, monitors, remembers. It never sleeps. Everything on the platform flows through it before reaching the user.

The animation must communicate this **without relying on large amounts of text**.

## Design mistakes to avoid

- Small object in the center of a large empty hero
- Glowing logo / DNA floating in void
- Scaling the helix larger instead of expanding the **environment**
- Decorative, random, or obviously looping motion
- Competing elements: graphs, product cards, dashboard previews, feature lists

## Environmental design

Think **digital ecosystem**. The visible core is only the heart; processing extends far beyond what the user immediately sees.

The background must feel **alive but never busy**:

- Very subtle particles (signals, not stars)
- Extremely faint neural connections
- Soft volumetric glow
- Slow orbital fields
- Intelligence waves, energy ripples, tiny moving signals
- Depth and atmospheric motion

Everything **slow, elegant, purposeful**. Nothing decorative. Nothing random.

## Scale (composition)

| Layer | Viewport share | Role |
|-------|----------------|------|
| Visible core (helix + BIE) | ~20–30% | Beating heart — original helix identity, not enlarged |
| Intelligence field (atmosphere, glow, rings, depth) | ~70–80% | The visitor is **inside** the system |

Do not make the object bigger. Make the **environment** bigger.

## Center

Dominates through **composition**, not scale: glow extends outward, ecosystem points inward, core breathes and reacts.

## Helix

Keep it — strongest visual identity. Treat as **living intelligence** (motion milestone): subtle twist, expand, contract, morph — thought, not mechanics. Never perfectly repeating.

## Intelligence rings

Not orbit rings or decorative circles. **Invisible energy layers** — different sizes, transparency, motion, rotation speeds. Some barely visible, some drifting, some pulsing. Organic field lines (magnetosphere / atom), not perfect circles.

## Particle story (later milestone)

Hundreds of signal particles. Most drift; occasionally one activates → glows → travels inward → helix responds → core brightens → ripple outward → calm. **Data enters → BIE validates → BIE produces intelligence.** No copy required.

## Motion (later milestone)

Nothing loops obviously. Asynchronous timings, different speeds, pauses, pulse intervals. Illusion of **living intelligence**, not a looping animation.

## Emotion

Visitors should feel: *this never stops · institutional · expensive · alive · validates everything · unlike any trading platform.*

Not: *this is a nice animation.*

## Inspiration (feeling only — do not copy)

Apple launches, JARVIS holograms, Interstellar higher-dimensional viz, Bloomberg precision, Palantir operational awareness, NVIDIA AI keynotes, particle accelerators, observatories, neural activity, magnetic fields, quantum simulations.

Scientific. Elegant. Institutional. Purposeful.

## Implementation milestones

| # | Milestone | Focus |
|---|-----------|--------|
| 1 | **Composition** | Viewport = intelligence field; layered atmosphere; 70–80% environmental fill; hierarchy toward core |
| 2 | **Motion** | Living helix; async, non-obvious loops; core breathe/react |
| 3 | **Particle system** | Signal drift + activation → core → ripple narrative |
| 4 | **Environmental effects** | Faint neural mesh; connections appear/disappear; depth |

**Rule:** Do not add more visual elements. Design a better **experience**.
